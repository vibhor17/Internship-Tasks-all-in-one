Virat Kohli

He was born in Delhi, India on 5 Nov ,1988. Virat is the first player in ICC cricket history to win all 3 ICC awards in a single year- ICC , ODI player of the year, ICC Test player of the year and ICC Player of the year award in 2018. Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who is known for his aggressive batting style and has been called one of the world's best batsmen.
A Symbol of Success and Hard Work: Kohli's success on the field and his entrepreneurial ventures have made him a symbol of success and hard work for the youth of India. He inspires young people to pursue their dreams and demonstrates that with dedication and perseverance, anything is possible.
